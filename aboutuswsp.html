Community Commitment

The 'Ride Like Wyatt' Grant Program

At WSP, we believe in giving back. Our flagship initiative, the 'Ride Like Wyatt' grant program, is dedicated to supporting aspiring young athletes in action sports. The program honors the spirit of Wyatt, a passionate rider whose legacy inspires us to foster talent and dedication.

Through this grant, we provide funding for equipment, training, and competition fees to help deserving individuals pursue their dreams. It's our way of investing in the future of the community we love.
